Exposed concrete, eometric forms, and utilitarian aesthetics, Brutal Brutalist Archive NY is a dedicated archive documenting the raw, monolithic beauty of Brutalist architecture across New York State.

Through photography, research, and curated insights, Brutalist Archive NY aims to foster appreciation for these bold, unapologetic structures and their place in New York's architectural history.
